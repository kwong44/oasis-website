Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Oasis

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. We don't sell your data or use it to train AI models without your consent.

Oasis analyzes your journal entries and suggests alternative language patterns that help rewire limiting thought patterns. Combined with personalized meditation soundbaths, you experience holistic transformation. For example, shifting "I have to" into "I get to experiment" changes your mindset from pressure to curiosity.

Oasis offers both free and premium tiers. Basic voice journaling is free, while premium unlocks personalized meditation soundbaths (3 per month), advanced AI insights, unlimited journal entries, and premium guided modules.

After completing journaling modules, Oasis analyzes your entries and generates personalized meditation scripts. ElevenLabs Music API creates professional soundbaths with AI narration and ambient music tailored to your transformation journey.

The Discovery Feed is an intelligent content system that surfaces personalized prompts, quotes, lessons, and modules. It tracks your exploration across 14 life categories and suggests weekly themes to guide your growth.

Modules are multi-step guided journaling experiences (5-15 minutes) that help you explore specific topics like gratitude, stress management, or self-compassion. Complete modules unlock personalized meditation soundbaths.

Yes, you can export your journal entries in various formats. Your data belongs to you, and you have full control over it including the right to download or delete it at any time.
